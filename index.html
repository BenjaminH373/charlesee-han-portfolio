Who is Charlesee Han?

Charlesee Han is a young author and publisher who has been creating stories since before he could spell “protagonist.” Armed with colored pencils, a wild imagination, and a pair of trusty headphones, Max has published 7+ books and shows no signs of slowing down.

When he’s not busy writing his next bestseller, you can find Max drawing, reading, or coming up with ideas for stories that are “way better than anything on TV.”

🎨 Loves drawing & art
🎧 Always has headphones on
📚 7+ published books
